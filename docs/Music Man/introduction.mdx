# Introduction
Music Man is a discord bot that allows you to play music in your discord voice channel. The bot is currently being rewritten and an announcement will go out in the discord when it is finished and this will be updated with more info.

If you encounter any issues using Music Man, [join the Discord](https://discord.gg/nDxrPWh8pk) and use one of the dedicated support channels to ask your question.
